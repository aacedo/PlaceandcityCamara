Let’s start!! – Please, read the following instructions carefully.

This is a web-map survey application for Lisbon city. We invite you to draw areas on the map freely.

To start, just press button at top-centre on the map part and then click on the map to define your first point and as many points as you need to define your area. To close the area, click on the first point again. You can edit the polygon moving any point at any time or delete it pressing button.

You can "pan" the map or zoom in (+ or -) using the map controls. You can draw as many areas as you need. The more areas you place, the better!
